Refund Policy

At Earning Skool, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase of courses or other content on our Services. If you are not satisfied with a course or other content, you may be eligible for a refund under certain circumstances.

Eligibility for Refunds

You may be eligible for a refund if you meet the following conditions:

  • You purchased the course or other content within the past 30 days.

  • You have not completed more than 50% of the course or other content.

  • You have not violated any of our terms and conditions.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us at support@earningskool.com and provide us with your name, registered email address, registration date, screen shot of payment invoice with date and time (You must have received on e-mail/message when you paid) and reason for refund. We will review your request and may ask for additional information to verify your eligibility for a refund.

Refund Process

If we approve your request for a refund, we will issue the refund to the original payment method used for the purchase within 5-7 business days. Please note that refunds may take longer to process depending on your bank or credit card company.

Refund Amount

The refund amount will depend on the purchase price of the course or other content, less any applicable discounts or promotions. We will not refund any fees or taxes paid in connection with the purchase.

Refund Cancellation

Once a refund has been issued, you will lose access to the course or other content for which the refund was issued. If you later decide to repurchase the same course or content, you will not be eligible for another refund.

Changes to Refund Policy

We may update this refund policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or to comply with applicable laws. If we make any material changes to this refund policy, we will notify you by email or by posting a notice on our website. Your continued use of our Services after any such changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised refund policy.

If you have any questions or concerns about this refund policy, please contact us at support@earningskool.com.
